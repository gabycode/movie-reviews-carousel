Movie Reviews

Big Hero 6

2015

Even though Hiro and Tadashi don't get the happily ever, this is still a story about the power of brotherly love, encouragement, and support. Baymax is the movie's most unique character, the perfect companion to young Hiro's jaded sense of doom about a world in which someone you love can be gone in a flash.

Brave

2012

This is a great-looking movie, much enlivened by the inspiration of giving Merida three small brothers, little redheaded triplets. The Scottish Highlands are thrillingly painted in astonishing detail, and some action shows Merida's archery more than equal in assorted emergencies.

Zootopia

2016

Zootopia is constantly asking its characters to look past species stereotypes, and not use species-ist language or repeat hurtful assumptions. Only a bunny can call another bunny 'cute,' Hops warns a colleague. It's filled with moments that are about overcoming or enduring discrimination.

Inside Out

2015

The script seems as if it's favoring Joy's interpretation of things, and what the other emotions ought to "do" for Riley. But soon we realize that Sadness has just as much of value to contribute, that Anger, Fear and Disgust are useful as well, and that none of them should be prized to the exclusion of the rest.
